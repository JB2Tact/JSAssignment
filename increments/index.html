How to Play Space Miner

Basics

Click the asteroid to mine minerals. Use your minerals to buy upgrades that help you mine faster and earn more per click.

Click Upgrades

These upgrades increase the number of minerals you earn per click:

  • Better Pickaxe — +1 mineral per click. Base cost: 15 minerals.
  • Laser Drill — +5 minerals per click. Base cost: 75 minerals.
  • Plasma Cutter — +25 minerals per click. Base cost: 500 minerals.

Auto-Miner Upgrade

This upgrade automatically mines minerals for you:

  • Mining Drone — First purchase starts auto-mining every 3 seconds. Each additional purchase speeds it up (faster interval). Base cost: 100 minerals.

Rewards

Earn badges by reaching milestones:

  • First Steps — Collect 100 minerals total.
  • Getting Rich — Collect 1,000 minerals total.
  • Space Tycoon — Collect 10,000 minerals total.
  • First Upgrade — Purchase your first upgrade of any kind.
  • Automation — Purchase your first Mining Drone.
  • Fully Loaded — Own 10 total upgrades.
  • Speed Demon — Purchase 5 Mining Drone upgrades.
